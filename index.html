About Us

Justitia Pax Et Veritas

DPSCMUN is an exciting Model United Nations conference being held at DPS Azaad Nagar. It is an opportunity for students to engage in global issues and learn about diplomacy, international relations, and negotiation. DPSCMUN is organized and run entirely by students, with the aim of fostering leadership, critical thinking, and teamwork skills.

The Participants will have the opportunity to represent various countries and debate a range of issues such as climate change, human rights, and security. Through simulated debates, students will learn about the workings of the United Nations and the importance of collaboration in addressing global challenges.

DPSCMUN is an inclusive conference that welcomes students of all backgrounds and experiences. Whether you are a seasoned MUN participant or new to the world of Model UN, DPSCMUN provides a supportive environment for learning, growth, and development.
